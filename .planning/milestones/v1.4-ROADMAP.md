## Overview

This milestone keeps scope intentionally narrow. The duration-management behavior shipped in v1.3 stays intact; the work here makes the `常亮时长` timed-duration area read as a clearly native macOS list and clarifies edit versus delete intent through semantic action styling without reopening duration persistence or root-menu truth.

## Milestone Summary

**Decimal Phases:**

- None

**Key Decisions:**

- Use a native SwiftUI `List` for the populated duration region instead of preserving the old `ScrollView`/`LazyVStack` shell.
- Keep edit/delete polish inside native accent/destructive button semantics instead of introducing heavier custom chrome.
- Stabilize the direct-launch add-sheet smoke with an accessibility-seam retry rather than weakening the verification boundary.

**Issues Resolved:**

- The duration manager no longer reads like a custom stacked surface; it now presents managed durations through a clearly native list.
- Edit and delete intent are now visually distinct at a glance while the existing CRUD callbacks stay intact.
- The add-sheet UI smoke no longer flakes on late sheet-marker exposure during verification reruns.

**Issues Deferred:**

- `CONV-04`: recent-device shortcuts remain deferred to a future milestone.
- `AWAKE-12`: one-off unsaved timed keep-awake durations remain out of scope.
- `AWAKE-13`: custom labels or notes for managed durations remain out of scope.
- `DIST-01`: packaging hardening such as signing or notarization remains a later milestone.

**Technical Debt Incurred:**

- No new runtime debt was introduced.
- A separate `v1.4-MILESTONE-AUDIT.md` was not created before archival; the milestone was archived from the passed phase verification report instead.
